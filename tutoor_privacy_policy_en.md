# Tutoor Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 20, 2026**

Thank you for choosing **Tutoor**. We deeply value the privacy of your data, and protecting it is our core principle. This policy explains how we handle your information.

---

## 1. Core Principles
Tutoor is an application built with **local storage at its core**.
* We do not maintain an account system.
* We do not collect, store, or upload any of your personal schedules or income data to our servers.
* We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

### 2.1 Data Logging
All data you input into Tutoor is **saved exclusively on your local iOS device**. As developers, we cannot access, read, back up, or modify this data.

### 2.2 iCloud Synchronization
To facilitate data synchronization across multiple devices, Tutoor utilizes Apple's **iCloud** service:
* Your data is encrypted and stored on Apple's cloud servers via your Apple ID.
* The developer does not own or have backend access to your iCloud container.
* You can disable iCloud sync permissions for Tutoor at any time in your iOS System Settings.

### 2.3 Subscription and Payment Data
Tutoor offers in-app subscriptions. All payment processes are handled through the Apple **App Store**:
* We **do not** obtain any of your payment methods (such as credit card info), real names, or contact numbers.
* We only receive anonymous transaction receipts provided by Apple to verify subscription validity and unlock premium features.

---

## 3. Security Measures
To ensure your data security, Tutoor employs the following mechanisms:
* **Local Storage:** Your data remains on your device.
* **iCloud Backup:** When iCloud is enabled, data is transferred to Apple’s encrypted cloud. Data is protected by Apple’s industry-leading security technology during transmission and storage.

---

## 4. User Rights
As the sole owner of your data, you have the following rights:
* **Right to Access:** You can view all entered data within the Tutoor app at any time.
* **Right to Manage:** You can modify or edit existing records whenever you choose.
* **Right to Erasure:** You can delete individual records at any time, or clear all locally stored data by uninstalling the app.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy
Tutoor is a productivity tool designed for teachers, training institution professionals, or freelancers.
* **Age Restriction:** Our services are **not directed to children under the age of 13**.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services
Tutoor is committed to providing a clean user experience:
* **No Ads:** We do not integrate any third-party advertising SDKs.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics tools (such as Firebase or Google Analytics) to track your in-app behavior.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly.
